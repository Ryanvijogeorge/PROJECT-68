campus life

School Chapel

Was established in June 1999

The chapel ensures a very peaceful and divine atmosphere for the students to offer their daily prayers and most of the students start their day at school with their prayers at the chapel.

School Library

Was built in June 1999

The state of the art library that is child friendly and that adheres closely to the green outdoors and provides our children with the right atmosphere and open up a world of make believe, that help our kids to develop listening skills and equip them with the necessary skills to become avid readers in future.

School Assembly Hall

Was built in June 1999

Assembly halls are meant to be a meaningful gathering place and we make every gathering a memorable one. Named after Saint Alphonsa, this 10,000 sq. feet space is a hosting venue for school assembly, Holy Mass and the major school day functions. It has a seating capacity of around 800.

school achievements

Cricket Champions
RCJPS team won the Kerala Cricket Association Tournament and more

RCJPS team won the Kerala Cricket Association Tournament, The Christu Jayanthi Tournament and the St Mary’s Public School Thamarachal Tournament. Krishna Prasad was adjudged the Man of the series in both the tournaments.

Football Champions
RCJPS footballers achieved a new level of success

RCJPS footballers achieved a new level of success by touching gold in all the tournaments they participated in. They were champions in the Sacred heart Soccer Cup, Thevara, the All Kerala football tournament, Village school, Thodupuzha, the Chavara Darshan Soccer Cup, Koonammavu, The Dawn International Football tournament, Palluruthy, the Santa Maria Soccer Cup, Mundamveli, the Bhavan’s Newsprint Soccer cup, Velloor, the Delta Soccer Cup, the Regional Sports Centre Soccer Tournament, Kadavanthra, the Bhavan’s Adarsha Cup, Kakkanad and the Toc–H Soccer Cup Vyttila etc.

Badminton Champions
Won the 12th Arch Bishop Joseph Attipetty memorial Badminton tournamen

RCJPS team was declared as the champions at the 12th Arch Bishop Joseph Attipetty memorial Badminton tournament held at Assissi Vidyaniketan, Kakkanad.
